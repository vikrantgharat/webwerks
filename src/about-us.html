About Our Organization

1996

Established in 1996

With the introduction of the Internet in India on August 15, 1995, by Videsh Sanchar Nigam Limited for the Indian public. It gave the opportunity to many entrepreneurs to spread their business wings globally. For making your business live on this platform called ‘Internet’ these business authorities needed to design and develop a website. This need was understood by Nikhil Rathi. As a result, Web Werks was established in 1996 in Mumbai, India. The prime vision of Web Werks was to provide a platform to Indian companies to develop and design websites in India only. Secondary vision to support the era of technological growth and innovations.

Data Center in US in 1997

Web Werks visioned to offer complete IT solutions for its clients from designing, developing to hosting as well. The fledgling company built its data center in Philadelphia, US. This data center was designed with the major objective to provide with an efficient facility which has the electrical and infrastructure of highest standards so as to ensure redundant power, network uptime, disaster control, secured, agile and scalable services to make significant contributions towards the growth of enterprises.

Introduced Co-location Services in 1999

E-commerce started emerging as a potent force for organizations as well as consumers. Technology linked buyers and sellers in new ways. As a result of the growth of e-commerce, Web Werks reported a remarkable growth in the business of data center in the US. Many private companies got added to the client list. Web Werks started planning of investing more in technology development and infrastructure. Since not all organization were able to afford to maintain an in-house data center, for them, Web Werks introduced co-location services. Also, one more USP got added to the service of Web Werks when they announced 24x7 Rapid Action Support to its customers.

Another Data Center in US in 2000

The growth rate of ‘data’ worldwide increased by 30%. Organizations were looking for faster Internet connectivity and non-stop operation to deploy systems and to establish a presence on the Internet. During this phase, Web Werks announced the launch of their second data center in Conshohocken. This data center offered carrier neutral, high availability and resilient Internet connectivity. Also, with multiple fiber providers available with diverse network connections.

Started with Indian Govt. Contracts in 2002

With the significant growth in the private sector. Web Werks turned his shift towards Government organizations. They also started signing up for the Indian Governments contracts. Presently they hold government sectors such as Mumbai Metro Rail Corporation Limited, Maharashtra Pollution Control Board, Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation, Nabard, and NPCI etc. Today, to offer best network connectivity Web Werks collaborated with many leading ISP's such as Tata Communications, Bharti Airtel, Reliance Communication, Tata Teleservices, Vodafone, Aircel, MTNL. The list is still adding on till date.

200 Employees Hired in a Month in 2004

Many corporate portals got added to Web Werks portfolio. But Web Werks client portfolio was not only the thing witnessing constant growth, it was even company’s workforce who witnessed the growth. Web Werks hired more than 200 employees in a single month. Customer faith in Web Werks solutions remains undaunted, and Web Werks increases its revenues once again by 37%. Moreover to this procurement of new infrastructure for future expansion was being planned.

Completed 10 Years in 2006

Web Werks reaches a milestone as it completes it’s 10 years in web hosting and data center business. During these 10 years, Web Werks show a remarkable and constant growth.
In the same year, Web Werks even built a data center in Mumbai keeping the Indian climatic and environmental conditions in mind. These data centers were the first data in India equipped with ‘Seismic Isolation Platform Technology' also known as ‘seismic base isolation’ or ‘base isolation system’ at our Data Centers to protect equipment against earthquake forces and other natural calamities.

New Data Center in Pune in 2008

Web Werks expanded it’s branch yet again within two years span! This time they set-up their second data center in Hinjawadi, Pune. It was originally set up as Near DR location for Mumbai Data Center. Even catering to regions closer to Pune. This data center was designed and developed especially keeping in mind mission-critical hosted products and services with a competitive edge.

800+ Employees hired in 2009

As the company got expanded it was time to expand the workforce again. 800+ people got added to the Web Werks family. These people were experts in their field and were hired to provide best customer satisfaction. Moreover, in the same year, Web Werks invigorates its solution portfolio with innovative new services and advancements including Converged Remote Infrastructure Management, Smart On-site, and a scope of new ClearPath improvements.

Big Partnerships Occurred in 2010

Web Werks was now a global leading brand. It was growing with time and now it was time for it to get into a partnership and some fruitful partnership occurred with Microsoft, Google, and Akamai. In the same year, Web Werks also met the rigorous security and protocol standards of HIPAA, PCI DSS, SSAE 16, SOC 2 and ISAE 3402. It was an astonishing achievement for Web Werks as it was now certified and secured.

Hold Multiple Govt. Contracts in 2012

By now, Web Werks became the first choice for data centers with many organizations. Web Werks even got accorded with the multiple Government contracts. Serving Government organizations were surely a milestone for Web Werks.
Web Werks also introduced Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM) equipment and programming bundle that can control an entire cluster of data center gear and IT assets from a solitary comfort. Also, Web Werks launched its Security ADV System for unbreakable data security.

TIER IV Data Center in Rabale in 2015

A silver year for Web Werks. As they launched the revolutionary Uptime Institute Certified data center in Rabale, Mumbai. Data center designed and built by IBM / Partners with one of Asia's largest Internet Exchange Hub. Its TIER IV data center was meant and understood to be the best in India. Racks were custom built by Rittal. Rittal is a German manufacturing company who expertise in server racks builds. Best infrastructure, most secured, latest hardware can be seen in this data center.
The company also announced its best quarterly performance ever in India, with more than 30 percent growth in data center revenue.

50% of Space Occupied in 2016

This is the year where Web Werks announced their Innovative Cloud Hosting Technology along with their Web Hosting services. Also, as per DCIM reports, Web Werks data centers spaces were occupied more than 50% already. Moreover, Web Werks also announced hosting all major DNS root servers in India as well.o with innovative new services and advancements including Converged Remote Infrastructure Management, Smart On-site, asyncd a scope of new ClearPath improvements.

Awarded with Multiple Awards in 2017

This was a golden year for Web Werks as it got awarded with so many recognized awards. Web Werks got awarded with 'The Best Web Hosting Award 2017', 'Retailer Of The Year For Best Cloud Data Centers And Internet Exchange', 'Great Indian Workplace Award (GIWA) 2017' awards. Also, Web Werks became the first data center to have OIX-2 and host an OIX-1 by Open-IX. MPCB Portal Developed by Web Werks received a Civil Service Award by Government of Maharashtra. Web Werks now is in the provisioning of their new Data Centers in Bangalore, Delhi and many other locations in India.
